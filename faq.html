Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Legisly.

What is Legisly?

Legisly is an AI-powered legislative intelligence platform that helps businesses, associations, and advocacy organizations track legislation, generate reports, and take action on bills that affect them.

Think of it as having a legislative analyst working for you 24/7 — monitoring thousands of bills, alerting you to changes, and helping you respond professionally.

Who is Legisly for?

Legisly is built for anyone who needs to track and influence legislation:

• Small and medium businesses affected by state regulations
• Trade associations representing industry interests
• Lobbying and government affairs firms
• Nonprofits and advocacy organizations
• Corporate government relations teams
• Law firms tracking regulatory changes

What states do you cover?

We're launching with California in Summer 2025, with Texas, New York, and Florida following shortly after. Our goal is full 50-state coverage plus federal legislation by 2026.

If you need a specific state, join the waitlist and let us know — we prioritize based on demand.

How does AI bill tracking work?

When you sign up, you tell us about your business in plain English. Our AI analyzes your description to understand your industry, concerns, and priorities.

Then it continuously monitors all bills in your covered states, automatically flagging the ones that could affect you. You get alerts when bills change status, approach deadlines, or require action.

How accurate is the predictive scoring?

Our AI predicts bill passage likelihood based on historical patterns, sponsor influence, committee dynamics, and political factors. In testing, our models achieve 75-85% accuracy on whether a bill will pass or fail.

Predictions improve as bills progress through the legislative process and more data becomes available.

Can I generate letters and testimony?

Yes! Legisly can generate professional support letters, opposition letters, committee testimony, and even amendment language — all customized to your position and concerns.

You review and edit before sending. The AI drafts, you control the final message.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your legislative positions and business information are never shared with other users or third parties.

See our Security page for more details on our practices.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime from your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

When is Legisly launching?

We're launching in Summer 2025, starting with California. Join the waitlist to be among the first to get access and receive updates on our progress.

How do I get in touch?

Email us at hadi@legisly.ai or visit our Contact page. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
