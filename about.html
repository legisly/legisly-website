About Legisly

Leveling the playing field in legislative advocacy.

Every year, thousands of bills are introduced that affect businesses of all sizes. Large corporations have armies of lobbyists tracking every move. Small businesses? They often don't know a bill exists until it becomes law.

That's not how democracy should work.

Our Mission

To democratize legislative intelligence so every business — regardless of size or budget — can participate meaningfully in the legislative process.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional legislative tracking is expensive, complex, and built for insiders. A full-service lobbyist costs $10,000-50,000 per month. Legislative tracking services charge thousands. Even if you can afford it, you need expertise to understand what you're looking at.

The result? 4 million small businesses in California alone go unrepresented. They find out about legislation that affects them too late — if at all.

Our Solution

Legisly uses AI to make legislative intelligence accessible to everyone. Tell us about your business in plain English, and our AI identifies every bill that could affect you. Get alerts when things change. Generate professional reports and letters with one click.

What used to require a team of analysts now takes seconds. What used to cost tens of thousands now costs $99/month.

Our Values

Accessibility

Legislative intelligence should be available to everyone, not just those who can afford lobbyists.

Transparency

We believe in clear pricing, honest communication, and showing our work.

Empowerment

Our job is to give you the tools and information to advocate for yourself.

Neutrality

We don't take political positions. We serve businesses across the political spectrum.

The Team

Legisly is built by a team with deep experience in legislative technology and AI. We've tracked legislation, built civic tech products, and seen firsthand how the system works — and who it leaves behind.

We're based in California and building for businesses nationwide.
